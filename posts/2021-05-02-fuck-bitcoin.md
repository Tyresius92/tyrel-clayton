title: "Fuck Bitcoin"
date: "2021-05-02"

Seriously, fuck Bitcoin. Fuck cryptocurrency.

But more than that, fuck all the people who have managed to convince the public that cryptocurrency is something that they can “invest” in. These people are liars - and what’s worse is that many of them don’t even realize that they’re lying.

Let’s get one thing straight. Cryptocurrency is NOT an investment. An interesting bit of technology? Sure. Something that may change the way in which money is transferred between two parties? Maybe...? I’m not in the business of making predictions. An investment? Fuck no.

Let’s talk about what an investment is. An investment is something that you put money, effort, or time into, with the reasonable expectation that you will generally get more out than you put in. Generally speaking, you are putting your money into something that has some intrinsic value - a company that produces goods or provides a service, or a house that you then rent out to provide passive income.

Sure, things can go wrong, and you may still lose money (the CEO may say something super racist and tank the company, or the house may burn down), but the underlying asset is expected to solve a problem for someone, somewhere, and THAT person is expected to be willing to part with their hard earned money.

In short, you can invest in things that provide value.

Now, let’s talk about what a gamble is. A gamble is something that you put money, effort, or time into, and then cross your fingers and hope that chance goes your way so you get more out than you put in. Generally speaking, you are putting your money on something that could reasonably be expected to go either way.

With a gamble, there’s nothing underneath which provides value. You just have to hope that the next card up is the five of hearts, or that the right ping pong ball comes out of the chute...or that the media buzz lasts long enough for you to sell your Bitcoin to someone else who heard “the price of that Bitcoin thing is going up!”

Bitcoin, in and of itself, doesn’t have any intrinsic value. There’s no problem that it solves outside of using it as a medium of exchange. You wouldn’t “invest” in the euro, or the yen, or the ouguiya, so why in the hell do you think that you can invest in cryptocurrency?

If you want to “invest” in Bitcoin, why don’t you cut out the middle man? Just head to Vegas, and put your money down on Black 13. The rest of us will be over here, building wealth.
